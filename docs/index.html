A little about us

Created with love and fueled by the joy of parenthood, Bobo was born from a delightful blend of technology and tenderness. Our inspiration comes from our own challenging experiences as first-time parents, the invaluable lessons we've learned, and the pure joy our son brings to our lives. Bobo is a testament to our dual passions - technology and problem-solving.

Co-founded by a tech-savvy couple as a portfolio project, we combined our expertise in software development and data analytics to create an intuitive, user-friendly app that simplifies the complex process of tracking a baby's milestones.

We've embedded advanced algorithms and user experience design principles to ensure every interaction with Bobo is seamless, rewarding, and, most importantly, fun. As we journey through the rewarding but challenging road of parenthood, we want to help fellow parents navigate it as well. We strongly believe in creating technology that resonates with our users and helps ease their burdens. Bobo is not just a milestone tracking app, it's a supportive community and a handy companion for every parent's journey.

Continuously learning and growing, we are constantly refining Bobo, ensuring it remains a reliable resource for parents. Our deep understanding of technology, coupled with our love for problem-solving, has allowed us to design a solution that encapsulates our experiences, our knowledge, and our hearts. Bobo isn't just a product of our technical expertise, but a labor of love, driven by our passion for nurturing our future generations. As parents, we understand your journey. As technologists, we're here to make it easier. Welcome to Bobo, where technology meets parenting.
